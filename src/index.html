How To Play

1. Press the 'START GAME' button on the main menu.
2. Use the arrow keys to move the shark around the screen.
3. Eat the fish to gain points and increase your health.
4. Avoid the mines and the jellyfish.
5. If you run out of health, you lose.
6. If you run out of hunger, you lose.
7. If you run out of time, you lose.
8. If you reach 1000 points, you win.
